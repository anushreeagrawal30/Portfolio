About Me
Born in the small town of Itarsi and now based in the bustling city of Bhopal, I am a driven entrepreneur and engineering student who thrives on innovation and challenges. With a Diploma in Production Engineering and currently pursuing a BTech in Electronics and Communication Engineering at LNCT Bhopal, I combine my technical expertise with my entrepreneurial spirit to create impactful solutions.

My journey into entrepreneurship has been one of resilience and determination. I’ve co-founded and worked with multiple startups, including BadBusiness, ACI, and Global Success Media, gaining invaluable experience in leadership and business development. Over the years, I’ve encountered and overcome hurdles such as startup idea rejections, communication challenges, introversion, and time management struggles. These experiences have shaped me into the leader I am today.

As the CEO of DevNBrand, I am committed to fostering innovation, driving growth, and empowering businesses with cutting-edge IT and marketing solutions. My journey began with a bold move to Bhopal to build a future founded on self-belief and vision.

I live by two E’s: Engineer by profession, Entrepreneur by passion.
My mission is to bridge the gap between technical expertise and business acumen to develop transformative solutions.
